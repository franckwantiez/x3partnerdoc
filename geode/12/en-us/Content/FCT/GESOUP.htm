Output movements

This screen is used to display an output movement.

Prerequisite

Refer to documentation Implementation

Screen management

  • Header
  • Tab General
  • Tab Controls

A movement no. is selected and the system displays its characteristics:

DO data. Number and status of the involved DO.

DO line data. Number, product and status of the associated DO line.

Movement data. Number and status of the output movement.

Pending transfer: if it is activated, this means that the DO to which the movement is linked is itself "pending transfer", i.e. it has been shipped to another site. On this site, the final shipment to the ship-to customer has no taken place yet.

Destination site: assigned if the flag "pending transfer" is activated. Specifies the site to which the movement has been shipped.

General tab. General data on the movement (Quantities, Dates, Lot, reservation and support numbers)

Management tab. Data on the addressing, preparation, validation, packing and shipping.

Remarks

An output movement is always associated with a single DO line.

The output movements are created by the system upon DO wave launching.

An output movement represents the smallest entity of a picking operation.

The output movements contain a status, they cannot be modified directly but their statuses and characteristics change with the DO processing steps.
