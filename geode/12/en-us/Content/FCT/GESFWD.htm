Deferments by parcel

This screen is used to partly defer the shipment of a packed DO, then to reintegrate it. The deferment is performed by Shipping Unit or by parcel, depending on the context.

This function is useful when the DO shipment must be performed in several phases, either because the final customer wants to split their delivery, or because the available space in the truck does not enable the whole DO to be loaded.

Within the framework of an "inter-site" shipment, it is possible to defer goods as long as the "inter-site" round to which it has been assigned has not been shipped. In other words, the parcel or SU cannot be defered if they are "pending transfer".

If the partial deferment of a DO with packing has been performed before shipment confirmation of the "inter-site" round, said partial deferment cannot be removed as long as the DO is "pending transfer".

The shipment deferment of an unpacked DO is performed by means of the Shipment deferment by DO function.

Unlike the Shipment Cancellation function, deferment means that the shipment is only postponed to a later date, and not totally deleted.

Available from the G2 version.

Screen management

The DO is selected, followed by the parcels or SUs to be deferred.

The DO status must be higher than Status 4-Pending preparation and must be in prepacking or declarative packing mode.

Parcel deferment: a single parcel can be deferred if it is neither palletized, nor loaded, nor shipped, nor already deferred, nor "pending transfer".

SU deferment: from the moment a selected parcel has been palletized, it is the SU that is deferred. The deferment of a SU consists in deferring all the parcels of the SU (including if the SU is multi-DO). A SU can be deferred open or closed if it is neither loaded nor shipped, nor "pending transfer".

Warning: the deferment of a multi-DO SU can cause the deferment of the parcels forming said SU (and as a consequence of the DOs).

Entry screen

For each parcel, such information as the SU no., the parcel no., its routing and its parcel type, its weight, its volume and its status.

If it is checked, the "Deferred" column specifies that the parcel line has been deferred or it enables the entry of the parcel to be deferred.

Report

Step 1: Enter the parcels or no. of the SU to be deferred.

Step 2: validate the action using the "Save" button.

Processes: If the selected parcel is not palletized, the parcel is updated with a deferred flag; the parcel movements all switch to status 7- Deferred upon shipment.

To partly defer a parcel, it is necessary to repackthe DO so as to isolate the movements to be deferred in an independent parcel.

If the selected parcel is palletized, it is the SU that is deferred, along with all the parcels that constitute it. Parcels are updated, along with the movements. The SU also contains a deferred flag.

To partly defer a SU, it is necessary to create a SU that only contains the parcels to be deferred.

A print-out is automatically generated to indicate the deferred parcels and SUs.

The DO can then be shipped and the SUs and/or parcels taken into account in the shipment will be those that are not deferred.

Reintégration

Step 1: Select the parcel or SU lines to be reintegrated for the shipment.

Step 2: validate the action using the "Save" button.

Processes: The parcels and SUs are updated (deferred flag set to No) and the corresponding movements move from status 7-Deferred upon shipment to status 8-Validated (initial status).

A print-out is automatically generated to indicate the deferred DO lines and quantities.

Error messages

  • Generic messages

In addition to the generic error messages, the following messages can appear during the entry :

Partial delivery prohibited.

The DO header set-up does not allow the DO to be shipped in a split fashion.

Multi DO SU.

Information that indicates that the SU to be deferred is multi DO. In case of confirmation, all the parcels of the DOs of the SU are deferred.

SU already loaded or shipped.

It is not possible to defer an SU already loaded or shipped.

Parcel already loaded or shipped.

It is not possible to defer a parcel already loaded or shipped.
