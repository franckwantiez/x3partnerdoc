Carriage Note

This document is enclosed with the goods upon shipment confirmation; it is the accurate reflection of a truck's load.
The CN must be given to the truck driver who takes the round as the evidence that the loading has been performed.

This report can be printed automatically upon shipment confirmation by round, if the setup of the round carrier includes the print code invoked.

The printout can be different by carrier code.

Prerequisite

  • Access to the report
  • List of criteria

Access to the report

This report can be accessed from the report menu of the group Outputs.

It is also called by default from the following functions :

  Outputs > Shipments > Carriage Notes

Description of the report

The header of the BT report displays the following information:

- Shipping site information: details of the site

- The information related to the round: Generic round, round no., round carrier code, round transport mode, round transport reference.

- The information related to the carrier: details of the carrier

- The loading information: Number of parcels, number of SUs, Gross volume, Gross weight, vehicle no. and seal no.

For each DN line, the report displays:

- The CR no. and the information related to the ship-to customer

- The DN nos. associated with the CR

- The number of parcels of the CR

- The weight and volume of each CR
