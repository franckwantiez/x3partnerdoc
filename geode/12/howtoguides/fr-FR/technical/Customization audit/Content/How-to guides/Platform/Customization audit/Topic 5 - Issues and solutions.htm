List of possible issues and solutions

The customization audit functions will be refined and evolve across time to reflect enhancements and evolutions in the Sage X3 development platform. This section is illustrative and should not be considered a definitive or exhaustive list of all possible risks, errors, and fatal errors.
The word specific below refers to custom elements developed using the classic Sage X3 development platform by partners, ISVs, developers, and customers, in opposition to standard elements delivered by Sage.
