In this table, “compatible” for browsers on Linux platform means that the OS/browser combination has passed tests with Sage X3 V12 on some variants of Linux but was not tested on all available combinations.

Other browsers (based or not on one the browsers mentioned above) may work with Sage X3 V12, but Sage does not certify other combinations.
